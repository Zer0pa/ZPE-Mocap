Reports with a reproducible case, a concrete artifact path, or a precise
command transcript will be triaged faster.

<p>
  <img src="../.github/assets/readme/section-bars/what-does-not-get-a-response-here.svg" alt="WHAT DOES NOT GET A RESPONSE HERE" width="100%">
</p>

- Security vulnerabilities opened publicly
- Claim disputes without counter-evidence
- Requests that should be routed to another repo or a private licensing channel

<p>
  <img src="../.github/assets/readme/section-bars/response-expectations.svg" alt="RESPONSE EXPECTATIONS" width="100%">
</p>

This is a source-available staging repo maintained by a small team. We aim to
acknowledge reports within 48 hours and triage within 7 days. Security reports
are prioritized.
