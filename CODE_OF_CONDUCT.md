We pledge to make participation in the ZPE-Mocap community a respectful,
evidence-first experience for everyone, regardless of background or role.

<p>
  <img src=".github/assets/readme/section-bars/our-standards.svg" alt="OUR STANDARDS" width="100%">
</p>

Expected behavior includes:

- Being respectful and professional.
- Focusing on reproducible evidence when challenging claims or behavior.
- Accepting constructive critique in service of truth and safety.
- Avoiding harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks.

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language.
- Publishing private data or credentials.
- Weaponizing evidence disputes as personal attacks.
- Repeatedly ignoring contribution or evidence rules after warnings.

<p>
  <img src=".github/assets/readme/section-bars/enforcement-responsibilities.svg" alt="ENFORCEMENT RESPONSIBILITIES" width="100%">
</p>

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards, resolving disputes,
and enforcing this code of conduct. Maintainers may remove or edit content
that violates these standards and may temporarily or permanently ban
contributors for unacceptable behavior.

<p>
  <img src=".github/assets/readme/section-bars/reporting.svg" alt="REPORTING" width="100%">
</p>

Report violations to `architects@zer0pa.ai`. Include the relevant context,
links, and any evidence that supports the report.

<p>
  <img src=".github/assets/readme/section-bars/enforcement-guidelines.svg" alt="ENFORCEMENT GUIDELINES" width="100%">
</p>

Enforcement decisions are made by maintainers with an evidence-first
standard. Reports that include concrete context will be handled faster.
